What’s Interesting About The Book?

The compelling narrative of Kaleb Bench’s encounters on Skinwalker Ranch during the production of the television show Secrets of Skinwalker can be found in Encounter in the Night. In the book, odd happenings, experiences with enigmatic phenomena, and his changing interpretation of the unexplained happenings at the ranch are all described. Kaleb’s adventure on Skinwalker Ranch is full of mystery and excitement, from seeing weird creatures to coming across strange floating objects with geometric patterns to going through personal failures. Insights into his spiritual beliefs, professional decisions, and life-altering effects of the unexplained phenomenon are revealed via his encounters. Gives readers a compelling look into the mysterious world of Skinwalker Ranch and his search for purpose and meaning amid the unsolved mysteries of the Uintah Basin.

What Keeps You Turn The Pages?

Readers are captivated and anxious to learn the truth about the bizarre happenings at Skinwalker Ranch because of the book’s captivating blend of mystery, suspense, and introspection. The novel has a captivating aspect that keeps readers turning pages because of the author’s detailed descriptions of his experiences, the spooky ranch setting, and the methodical solving of the problems. In addition, the novel is made more complex by the author’s introspective trip and the examination of deeper issues like spirituality and the unknown, which entices readers to read on. Encounter in the Night is an engrossing read that won’t let up until the very end, thanks to a riveting blend of intriguing storytelling, mysterious events, and human development.

Author

Kaleb Bench

Kaleb Bench grew up in a small farming community near Blackfoot, Southeastern Idaho, where he actively participated in sports like football and basketball and helped with farming potatoes, hay, and grain. After graduating from Snake River High School, Kaleb enlisted in the United States Marine Corps as an M1A1 Main Battle Tank crewman, serving six years in the Reserves before receiving an Honorable Discharge. He attended Utah State University, where he played football and rugby while studying ancient Greek and Roman history, mythologies, Latin, German, and World War II. In his final year, Kaleb joined the family business with his two brothers at his father's request. After his father's death from cancer, Kaleb faced personal and professional challenges but found solace and strength through a profound faith in Jesus Christ. This period also led him to explore self-improvement, learning from leaders like Jim Rohn, Tony Robbins, and Bob Proctor. Kaleb met and married Stephanie, and they moved to Northeastern Utah, where they are proud parents of four children. In the Uintah Basin, Kaleb became a Law Enforcement Officer and Security Consultant for the renowned Skinwalker Ranch. He has been involved in the scientific investigation of the ranch for over seven years, contributing to the television show “The Secrets of Skinwalker Ranch.” Kaleb's unique experiences on and off the ranch provide valuable insights into the phenomenon and its broader implications, which he eagerly shares to help others through relatable experiences.
